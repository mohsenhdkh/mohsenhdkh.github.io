Mohsen Heidari

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Center for Science of Information (CSoI) at Purdue University working with Prof. Wojciech Szpankowski. I received my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Sandeep Pradhan in Electrical Engineering and my M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Michigan in 2018 and 2017, respectively. I received my M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology, Iran, in 2013 and 2011, respectively. My research interests broadly lie in information theory, quantum information theory and statistical learning. For more information please find my CV.
